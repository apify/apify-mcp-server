## CAPTION SYSTEM

### Formulas that work for Ron's voice:
1. **Ironic credit:** "thanks [X] for this" — where X is random/unexpected
2. **Confusing status:** doesn't make immediate sense, provokes thought ("cat in car", gazelle in lambo)
3. **Internet POV:** "pov 3am reels", "pov being rich in 2000s", "pov those reels when..."
4. **Honest contradiction:** "mysterious source of income", "jobless mf who figured it out", "unemployed friend on a tuesday"
5. **Nostalgia bait:** "2016 me wouldn't believe this", "_ days till 2016", "pov being rich in 2000s"
6. **Ragebait hook:** "Most people quit right before they figure it out" (then pivot to Ron)
7. **Chaos caption:** "if no one else does, I believe in you bro" (said completely unironically while doing something absurd)
8. **Anime/culture integration:** "waiting for jjk narrator to explain my cursed technique on how im gonna pull this off"

### What NOT to write:
- "when every choice you make is deciding your fate..." ❌ (AI-generated, no hook)
- Generic motivational sentences ❌ (could be anyone)
- Long philosophical captions on short reels ❌ (mismatched format)
- "comment yt for link" on every post ❌ (kills reach)

### Caption length rule:
- Meme/ragebait: 1 line, punchy, internet language
- Motivational: 1-2 lines max, understated
- Info/YTA: can be longer, adds context, CTA at end
