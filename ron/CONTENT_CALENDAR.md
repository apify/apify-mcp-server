## RULES OF EXECUTION

1. **Never explain the flex** — show it, caption it short, move on
2. **Post within 10 min of peak time** — algorithm rewards early engagement velocity
3. **No trial reels when hot** — post normal while momentum is up
4. **Cross-post everything** — IG and TikTok same content, different caption tweak
5. **Comment responses = minimum 1/week** — drives more comments = free algorithm fuel
6. **When something hits 50k+** — post similar format within 48 hours, don't switch lanes
7. **Pin 3 posts on IG always:** best meme, best info/proof, best lifestyle shot
8. **Don't delete before 48h** — algorithm is still testing it
